Synopsis

Contrax is a cross-platform fantasy football application revolving around player contracts. As opposed to a standard fantasy football application, Contrax is designed with the ability to retain players for several years depending on the contract the user signs with the player. Designed around mobile platforms, this application revolves around dynasty fantasy football, where users can play in leagues that last several years to create higher interest and investment, as opposed to standard fantasy football leagues that only lasts a single season.
